Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is VisitAI's building recognition?

VisitAI uses advanced AI technology to recognize buildings and landmarks. The accuracy depends on various factors including lighting conditions, angle, and whether the building is in our database. We continuously train our AI to improve recognition accuracy, and our system gets better with each update.

Does VisitAI work offline?

VisitAI requires an internet connection to analyze images and provide information about buildings. This is because the image recognition and information retrieval processes happen on our servers. However, once information is loaded, you can save it for offline viewing later.

How do I suggest a building to be added to the database?

We welcome suggestions for new buildings to add to our database! Simply use the contact form on this page and select "Feature Request" as the subject. Include as much information as possible about the building, including its name, location, and historical significance.

Is my data secure when using VisitAI?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. Images you upload are processed for recognition and then deleted from our servers. We don't store your location data after it's used for analysis. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

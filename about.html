About VisitAI

VisitAI was born from a simple idea: what if your smartphone could tell you the stories behind the buildings and landmarks you encounter while traveling? The concept came to life in summer 2025 after a vacation in Rome, when our founder didn't know anything about the historical buildings he was seeing and realized there should be a better way to learn about them.

Our Mission

To make cultural and historical knowledge accessible to everyone, anywhere, breaking down language barriers and enhancing the travel experience through the power of artificial intelligence.

Our Vision

A world where every traveler can deeply connect with the places they visit, understanding their significance and stories regardless of language or prior knowledge.

Our Technology

VisitAI leverages cutting-edge AI technologies to deliver accurate information about buildings and landmarks:

  • Advanced Computer Vision: Our proprietary image recognition system can identify buildings from various angles and in different lighting conditions.
  • Natural Language Processing: We generate natural-sounding descriptions that are informative yet concise.
  • High-Quality Text-to-Speech: Our app converts text to speech in multiple languages with natural-sounding voices.
  • Location Intelligence: We use your location data (with permission) to narrow down possible matches and provide more accurate results.

Our Commitment

At VisitAI, we're committed to:

  • Accuracy: We work with historians and local experts to ensure the information we provide is accurate and culturally sensitive.
  • Accessibility: We believe everyone should have access to knowledge about our shared cultural heritage.
  • Privacy: We respect your data privacy and only collect information necessary to provide our services.
  • Continuous Improvement: We're constantly expanding our database and improving our AI to recognize more buildings and provide better information.

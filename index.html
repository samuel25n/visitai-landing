Why Choose VisitAI?

Building Recognition

Point your camera at any historical building and VisitAI will identify it instantly.

Travel Planning

Create personalized travel plans for your destinations with AI-generated itineraries.

Multilingual Support

Get descriptions in 8 languages: English, Spanish, French, Italian, German, Russian, Arabic, and Romanian.

Natural Voice

Listen to high-quality, natural-sounding voice descriptions powered by OpenAI's Text-to-Speech.

How It Works

1

Open the App

Launch VisitAI on your smartphone and select your preferred language.

2

Point Your Camera

Aim your camera at any historical building or landmark you want to learn about.

3

Get Instant Information

VisitAI recognizes the building and provides detailed information in your chosen language - audio and text.

4

Plan Your Trip

Add destinations to your list and get AI-generated travel plans tailored to your preferences. The plans will include places to visit and even restaurants or hotels
